The Intuition-Led Parent

You trust your instincts, and they're right. You keep it focused on what matters: knowing your baby, not the numbers.

The archetype

The one your baby calms down for first.

You parent from the body, not the book.

Before you read a sleep method, you already knew when your baby was tired. Before you read about cluster feeding, you already adjusted. You are wired for this and it is embarrassing how good you are at it.

Your love language is attunement.

You meet them where they are. You do not try to schedule the unschedulable. You read the room, the cry, the silence, and you respond with a calm that feels like home to them.

When it gets hard, you soften.

You do not escalate with them. You absorb the storm and stay warm. Parents who track everything would envy the fluency you already have. They just do not know it yet.

The flaw that is not

People think you are relaxed. You are actually highly tuned in.

You do not need a spreadsheet to know your baby. That is not laziness. That is the expert level most tracking parents are secretly trying to reach. You got there by being present.

Your parent twin

Your parent energy: the old soul.

Quietly confident. Naturally warm. The kind of parent other parents watch at the park and think, "how do they make it look so easy?"

How Pippy assigns this type

You get this type when you log what matters and skip the rest. Pippy notices the rhythm of a parent who is already fluent in their baby.

Pippy does not ask you to fill out a quiz. Your parenting personality comes from the way you already log feeds, sleep, diapers, and milestones. After a few days of logging, your type appears in the Fun tab of the app, with a shareable card.

A closing note

Your baby does not know the word "instinct" but they feel yours in every hold.
