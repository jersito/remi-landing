Clinical Data Points

The data that matters for assessment.

Every summary includes structured data across six clinical categories, with multi-day trends and drill-down capability.

🍼

Feeding Trends with Type Split

Daily feed counts with breast vs. bottle breakdown over 7 and 30 days. Track supplementation trends, cluster feeding patterns, and frequency changes across the reporting period.

💧

Hydration Monitoring

Wet diaper counts tracked daily as a key dehydration indicator. Especially critical for newborns where output below 6 wet diapers/day warrants assessment. Stacked charts show wet vs. stool separately.

💤

Sleep Architecture

Total sleep hours, nap count and distribution, and overnight consolidation patterns. Identify whether sleep is fragmented across short naps or consolidating into age-appropriate blocks.

📝

Stool Logging with Notes

Parents log color, consistency, and freeform notes with each diaper change. Useful for GI assessment, formula transition monitoring, and identifying patterns that may indicate intolerance.

⚠️

Guideline-Based Alerts

Automatic comparison against AAP and AASM age-appropriate benchmarks for feeding frequency, diaper output, and sleep totals. Flagged concerns appear prominently with the relevant threshold cited.

🌡️

Health Event Timeline

Temperatures, symptoms, and medications logged in chronological context alongside feeding and sleep data. See how an illness event correlates with changes in intake, output, and rest patterns.
