Smart Alerts

Guideline-based health flags.

Remi checks your baby's data against established AAP and AASM guidelines for their age. If something falls outside the typical range, it gets flagged so your doctor knows what to look at.

  • ✓ Diaper output below threshold for age
  • ✓ Feed frequency below recommended range
  • ✓ Sleep totals outside typical ranges
  • ✓ Age-appropriate thresholds (0-6mo, 6-12mo, 12-24mo)
⚠️
Concerns
● Wet diapers averaging 4.2/day. Under 6 months, AAP recommends at least 6 wet diapers per day.
● Sleep averaging 10.5 hours/day. AASM recommends 12-16 hours for infants 4-12 months.

Based on AAP/AASM guidelines. Not medical advice.
