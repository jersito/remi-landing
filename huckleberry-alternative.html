FAQ

Common questions

Is Pippy really free?
Yes. Pippy's core features including AI chat logging, family sharing, nap predictions, and pediatrician-ready reports are in the free tier. Pippy Premium ($49.99/year) adds image attachments, AI pattern insights, multiple babies, and unlimited log history.
Does Huckleberry have voice logging?
Huckleberry recently added AI logging that supports text, voice, and photo inputs, but it requires the Huckleberry Plus subscription at $58.99 per year. Pippy's AI chat logging is in the free tier.
Can I track multiple babies in Pippy?
Tracking multiple children requires Pippy Premium. Huckleberry allows multiple children on the free tier. If that's important to you and you don't want to pay, Huckleberry may be the better fit for now.
Is family sharing free in Pippy?
Yes. Sharing your baby's timeline with your partner, nanny, or grandparents is free on both Pippy and Huckleberry.
Can I import my Huckleberry data into Pippy?
Not automatically today. You can manually log recent days in Pippy to get started. If this is something you'd need, let us know at our contact page and we'll flag demand.
