Stop arguing about dinner

Munch is the fastest way to figure out what you and your partner actually want to eat. Swipe through 35 cuisines. The instant you both like the same one, both phones celebrate. Done. Order food.

How it works

Open the app on both phones, on the same Wi-Fi. Each phone shows up on the other automatically — no QR codes, no logins, no waiting room. Tap to invite, accept, swipe at the same time. A match splash pops the moment your "yes" lines up with theirs.

Not on the same Wi-Fi?

Send a snapshot of your cravings as a deep link. Your partner opens it and sees a comparison view of where the two of you overlap.

What it doesn't do

It doesn't have servers. It doesn't have accounts. It doesn't track you, show ads, or send anything to the cloud. Live couples mode is peer-to-peer over your local Wi-Fi only — nothing leaves the network. Snapshot shares are a deep link in your phone's share sheet, so we never see them either.
