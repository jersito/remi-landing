The Honest Version

How we stay free

The honest version

We're not going to pretend Pippy costs nothing to run. That's not real and we don't want to lie.

The core stays free. AI chat logging, family sharing, nap predictions, and doctor reports are free and we plan to keep them that way. Those are basics, not extras.

For parents who want more, Pippy Premium at $49.99 per year adds image attachments, AI pattern insights (smart summaries of trends in your baby's data), multiple babies, smarter AI, and unlimited history.

We'd rather lots of parents use Pippy free than a small group pay for things that should have been free.

FAQ

Common questions

Is Pippy really free?
Yes. AI chat logging, family sharing, nap predictions, and doctor reports are all free. No credit card needed.
What do I get with Pippy Premium?
Pippy Premium ($49.99/year) adds image attachments, AI pattern insights, tracking more than one baby, smarter AI, and unlimited history.
Is the 30-day history limit a dealbreaker?
For most parents, no. Doctor visits, partner sharing, and weekly patterns all fit in 30 days. If you want the full history, that's what Premium is for.
How does the free tier compare to Huckleberry's free tier?
Pippy's free tier has AI chat logging and nap predictions. Both of those need Huckleberry Plus ($58.99/year). Huckleberry's free tier lets you track more than one baby and keeps a longer history. Both of those need Pippy Premium.
