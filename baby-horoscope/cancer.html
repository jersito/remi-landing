The weekly Cancer baby horoscope

Inside the Pippy app, your Cancer baby gets a fresh horoscope every Monday. It blends the traditional qualities of their sign with the quiet rhythms of your week, the busiest day, the longest stretch of sleep, the hour the two of you seem to orbit, all pulled anonymously from your logs.

The card includes a short forecast, a three-day horizon of day-by-day predictions, a lucky hour, and a gentle ritual suggestion. Day-by-day predictions roll forward as the week goes on, so it stays fresh from Monday through Sunday.

What makes a Cancer baby's horoscope different

Cancer babies share certain tendencies, but the horoscope is written to your specific little one. If your Cancer had a long sleep on Tuesday, or the day shifted around a feeding peak, the reading notes it. The sign gives the flavor. Your logs give the detail.
