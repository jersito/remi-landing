Signs your baby is getting enough

💧

Wet diapers

After day 5, 6 or more heavy wets in 24h. Pale yellow urine.

💩

Regular poops

3 to 4 stools a day early on. Breastfed can skip days after 6 weeks.

📈

Weight gain

Back to birth weight by 2 weeks, then 5 to 7 oz per week.

😌

Content after feeds

Hands relax, body softens, comes off on their own.

👂

Swallowing

Audible gulps on breast. Steady swallows on bottle, not frantic.

🌱

Steady growth

Trending in their own curve lane at checkups.

How to use this feeding tracker

A feeding log tells you when the last feed happened when your brain is too tired to remember, spots cluster feeding before it feels random, and gives your pediatrician real numbers instead of guesses. This tracker stores every entry in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.

Breastfeeding: log the minutes

Pick breast left, breast right, or both sides, and enter total minutes. Newborn feeds run 20 to 45 minutes. Older babies get more efficient and feeds often shrink to 10 to 20 minutes. That is normal, not a problem.

Bottle feeds: log oz or ml

Pick bottle for breast milk in a bottle, formula for formula, or pumped milk if you want to track pumping output separately from intake. Use the note field for leftovers, spit-up, or a bottle refusal.

Cluster feeding is normal

Back-to-back feeds for several hours, usually evening, often before a longer night stretch. Most intense around 2 to 3 weeks, 6 weeks, and 3 months. Exhausting but normal.

When to call your pediatrician

  • Fewer than 6 wet diapers in 24 hours after the first week.
  • Baby has not regained birth weight by two weeks old.
  • You cannot wake your baby for a feed, or baby is sleeping through every feed for more than 4 to 5 hours in the first month.
  • Signs of dehydration: sunken soft spot, no tears when crying, very dark urine, dry mouth.
  • A sudden, lasting drop in feed volume that is not a growth spurt or teething.
  • You are worried. You know your baby. Call.

Privacy

Your feeds are saved in this browser's localStorage. They never touch a server. Clearing your browser data or using private mode will wipe the log, so tap Download PDF before switching devices.
