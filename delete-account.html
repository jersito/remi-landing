Remove your Pippy account and all associated data

Last updated: April 12, 2026

This page lets you request deletion of your Pippy account and all of its data. You do not need to have the app installed to use this page. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Option 1: Delete From Inside the App (Fastest)

The quickest way to delete your account is from inside Pippy:

  • Open Pippy on your phone
  • Go to Settings
  • Scroll to Privacy & Data
  • Tap Delete my data (removes only your data) or Delete household data (removes everything for your household — owner only)
  • Confirm when prompted

Your data is removed from our servers immediately.

Option 2: Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account and data deletion by emailing us:

Email: support@meetpippy.com

Please include: your account email, approximate date you created the account, and the phrase "I am requesting deletion of my Pippy account and all associated data." We process these requests within 30 days.

Option 3: Submit the Form Below

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Your account profile and email
  • All baby tracking entries (feeding, sleep, diaper, pumping, growth, health notes, milestones, general notes)
  • Photos you uploaded, including AI-analyzed photos
  • Chat history with the Pippy assistant
  • Achievements, insights, reminders, and visit summaries
  • Referral codes and linked household invites
  • Push notification tokens

What May Be Retained

A small amount of data may be retained for a limited time for legitimate reasons:

  • Anonymous, aggregated analytics — these contain no personal identifiers and are not tied to your account.
  • Subscription / payment records — held only as required by tax, accounting, or fraud-prevention laws. These are not linked to your personal profile after deletion.
  • Security and abuse-prevention logs — retained briefly as needed to protect the service.

Subscriptions: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active subscription. To stop future charges, cancel via Google Play Subscriptions (Android) or App Store Subscriptions (iOS).

Timeline

Requests submitted through the app are processed immediately. Email and form requests are processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete.

Questions

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at support@meetpippy.com.
