Delete Your Account

Remove your Pippy account and all associated data

← Back to Pippy
Last updated: April 12, 2026

Pippy uses anonymous accounts. We do not collect your name, email, or phone number. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

How Pippy identifies you: when you first open the app, we generate a random ID on your device and use it to store your baby logs. There is no email, username, or password. The only place your account exists is on the device where you installed it.

Best way: delete from inside the app

If you still have Pippy installed, this is the only reliable way to delete your data:

  • Open Pippy on your phone
  • Go to Settings
  • Scroll to Privacy & Data
  • Tap Delete my data (removes only your data) or Delete household data (removes everything for your household, owner only)
  • Confirm when prompted

Your data is removed from our servers immediately.

If you no longer have the app

Because accounts are anonymous, we cannot look up your data from an email address or phone number. That information is never attached to it in the first place. If you've already uninstalled Pippy without deleting your data first, please email us anyway:

Email: support@meetpippy.com

Include any details you remember (approximate date you created the account, your baby's name, the device you used). We'll do our best to locate and remove the data, and we'll write back either way within 30 days.

Practically, an anonymous account with no linked device has very little data left to find, since there are no contact details or photos of you on file, but we will make a good-faith effort.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • All baby tracking entries (feeding, sleep, diaper, pumping, growth, health notes, milestones, general notes)
  • Photos you attached to log entries
  • Chat history with the Pippy assistant
  • Achievements, insights, reminders, and visit summaries
  • Household membership and referral codes
  • Push notification tokens
  • Your anonymous account row

What may be retained

A small amount of data may be retained for a limited time for legitimate reasons:

  • Anonymous, aggregated analytics. Contains no identifiers that could tie events back to you.
  • Subscription / payment records. Held only as required by tax, accounting, or fraud-prevention laws. Payment information itself never reaches Pippy; it is handled by Google Play or the App Store.
  • Security and abuse-prevention logs. Retained briefly as needed to protect the service.

Subscriptions: Deleting your data does not automatically cancel an active subscription. To stop future charges, cancel via Google Play Subscriptions (Android) or App Store Subscriptions (iOS).

Timeline

In-app deletion is immediate. Email requests are processed within 30 days.
