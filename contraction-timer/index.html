A calmer contraction timer

Most contraction apps flash ambulances at you and push you toward the hospital. Pippy doesn't. It keeps a quiet, accurate log, shows your average duration and spacing, and gently lets you know when your pattern matches a common rule of thumb (5-1-1 or 4-1-1, your choice).

What it does

Times each contraction, remembers the gap between them, shows your running average, and builds a short text summary you can share with your partner or midwife. Long-press any row to remove it if you tapped by mistake. Undo is always one tap away.

What it doesn't do

It doesn't diagnose. It doesn't tell you to go to the hospital. It doesn't send your data anywhere. It doesn't show you ads or charge you. It doesn't pretend to be a medical device.
