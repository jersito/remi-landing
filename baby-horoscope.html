How Pippy writes a weekly baby horoscope

We start with the traditional traits of your baby's zodiac sign, then layer in the quiet rhythms of your week. The busiest day, the longest stretch of sleep, the hour you and baby seem to orbit. All anonymously pulled from your logs, never shared.

The result is a short, warm weekly reading with a three-day forecast and a lucky hour. Light enough to be fun. Specific enough to feel like it was written for your baby.

Readings refresh every Monday. Day-by-day predictions roll forward so the card never feels stale mid-week.
